Render Seats for a Venue in an SVG

The seats#index template iterates over the available sections, rendering <g> elements, then iterating over each Section record’s seats and rendering <circle> elements as children into the groups. The Seat records are rendered as <svg> elements that are positioned with [x] and [y] attributes from the Seat record’s x and y attributes.

Additionally, dynamically render the name of the Venue.

Class names correspond to the pre-declared styles.
